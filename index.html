Our Story

of how it all started
One afternoon, a few years ago in Taiwan, the founder of this bakery, Mr. Wu Cheng-Hsueh took a group of business associates to a cafe in a local five-star hotel to celebrate their success. Peering through the window, he saw that people outside of the cafe were busy and rushed, whereas the patrons inside the cafe were having a relaxed moment enjoying gourmet desserts and drinks. Upon opening the menu, he realized the fact that the prices were set way too high for ordinary people to enjoy on a whim. At that moment, Mr. Wu envisioned a cafe that provides five-star quality coffee, cakes, and breads at not so five-star prices. Thus, the journey begins...
